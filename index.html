If Premier League teams are level on points, the order of tiebreakers to determine league position is:

  1. Goal difference
  2. Goals scored
  3. Head-to-head points
  4. Head-to-head away goals
  5. A one-off playoff game at a neutral venue (only for deciding titles, European qualification, or relegation)

Note: In Advanced mode, positions are determined by the first 2 tiebreakers (goal difference and goals scored).
